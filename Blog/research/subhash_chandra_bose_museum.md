# Subhash Chandra Bose Museum (Netaji Birth Place Museum) - Research

## Historical Background
- **Location**: The museum is located in Janakinath Bhawan, the ancestral house of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose, in Cuttack, Odisha.
- **Significance**: Subhas Chandra Bose was born in this house on January 23, 1897, and spent his early childhood here.

## Exhibits
- **Galleries**: The museum has 12 galleries showcasing different phases of Netaji's life.
- **Collections**: The museum features a collection of original letters written by Netaji, photographs, personal belongings, and books related to his life and the Indian freedom struggle.

## Location Details
- **City**: Cuttack, Odisha.
- **Address**: Oriya Bazar, Cuttack.
