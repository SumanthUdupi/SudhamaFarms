Architecture & Design

Built with locally sourced Laterite stone, the farmhouse embodies zero-mile construction. Its design respects the local climate with natural cross-ventilation and traditional clay tiles, creating a space that breathes with the land.

Sustainable Practices

Soil Health

We enrich our soil using only manure from our own cows and compost prepared on the farm.
